Privacy Policy

Password Manager App  ·  Last updated: January 2025  ·  Effective immediately

🔒 Short version: Your passwords never leave your device unless you choose to back them up to your own Google Drive. We collect nothing. We store nothing on any server.

1. Who We Are

This Privacy Policy applies to the Password Manager web application available at https://jaisonjai29.github.io (the "App"). The App is developed and maintained as a personal project.

2. Data We Collect

We collect no personal data whatsoever. Specifically:

  • We do not collect your name, email address, or any personal identifiers
  • We do not collect your passwords or any data you enter into the App
  • We do not use analytics, tracking pixels, or cookies
  • We do not have any servers that receive or store your data
  • We do not serve advertisements

3. Where Your Data Is Stored

All data you enter — including app names, usernames, and passwords — is stored exclusively in your browser's localStorage on your own device. This storage:

  • Stays on your device only
  • Is accessible only by this app on this device
  • Is never transmitted to any server by this app
  • Is deleted if you clear your browser data or uninstall the browser

4. Google Drive Backup (Optional)

The App offers an optional feature to back up your data to your personal Google Drive account. This feature:

  • Is entirely opt-in — you must manually initiate a backup
  • Requires you to sign in with your own Google account
  • Encrypts all data with AES-256-GCM encryption on your device before uploading — we cannot read your data even if we wanted to
  • Stores the backup only in your own Google Drive — not in any storage we control
  • Uses Google's OAuth 2.0 — we never see your Google password

When you use Google Sign-In, Google may share your name, email address, and profile picture with this App. This information is stored only in your browser's localStorage and is used solely to display your profile within the App. It is never transmitted to any third-party server.

Your use of Google Drive is subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

5. Encryption

Before any data is uploaded to Google Drive, it is encrypted using AES-256-GCM with a passphrase you choose. The encryption happens entirely on your device. The passphrase is never stored anywhere — only you know it. Without your passphrase, the encrypted backup is unreadable by anyone including us.

6. Third-Party Services

The App loads fonts from Google Fonts. Google Fonts may log the request to their CDN. See Google's Privacy Policy. No other third-party services are used.

7. Children's Privacy

This App is not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the new policy.

9. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or the App's data practices, you can reach us at the GitHub repository linked from the App, or by opening an issue at github.com/jaisonjai29.
